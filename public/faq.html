FAQ

Get know in detail about BeAChef

1.How can I upload the image of my recipe?

You can be able to capture it with camera in the app or can upload it from saved photos.


2.Can I like my own recipes?

You can be able to capture it with camera in the app or can upload it from saved photos.


3.Can I share the recipe with my friends?

Yes, you can share your recipes with your friends by sharing it in social networking apps like Facebook etc.


4.How can I make the recipe as my favorite?

You can make recipe as your favorite by liking it.


5.Can I invite friends to join the app?

Yes, you can invite your friend from the option “Tell A Friend”.


6. Can I report on the recipe post if it is a spam?

Yes, you can click on report option and select that it is a spam. We will verify and delete the recipe post.
